Moving to Portland

I moved to Portland in Late September, 2014. I wanted to get out of the cold of Alaska. I used to live in Oregon and I visited Portland once before. It's a stylish looking town. It's a little bit weird and I like that.

Finding an Apartment

My sister was nice enough to let me stay at here place while I searched for an apartment. I woke up early every morning to call apartments. I had actually been calling apartments for months while I was still in Alaska. I finally got a call after about a month about a possible opening. An apartment I had called was nice enough to call me and tell about it.

Doing things in Portland
